About Us
Export House India has managed to create its own niche in the evergrowing Indian Handicraft Sector. Manufacturing and Exporting home decor accessories all over the world, bringing in new styles and raising the bar in terms of quality and innovation. In all these years, we have gained a vast experience in a variety of mediums viz. Brass, Iron & Aluminium metalware. We supply to all parts of the world including Western Europe, USA, Japan, Brazil & South East Asia. Our clients are mainly Whole salers and Retailers.
